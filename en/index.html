FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Service area, payment, warranty and more — the questions we hear most. Anything else? Just ask us on LINE.

Service area

  • Within Osaka City — the yearly plan includes free delivery, setup and end-of-term collection
  • We also serve Nara, Kyoto and Hyogo (Kobe)
  • Outside the city, delivery is ¥8,000–¥18,000 depending on distance and whether there's an elevator
  • End-of-term collection is free as well

Rental term

  • From one month minimum — the yearly plan is the best value
  • You can extend after the term, prorated by the number of extra months

Payment

  • Card only — no instalments, no cash on delivery
  • From overseas you can book first; we arrange the delivery date once payment is complete

Delivery hours

  • 10:00–16:00 (no specific time slot; by appointment)

Warranty

  • Faults from normal use during a yearly rental → free repair or replacement
  • User-caused damage → chargeable (at KUMAGO's discretion)

Going home early

  • Returning early costs nothing extra

Cancellation & refunds

  • After payment, no cancellation or refund except for force majeure such as natural disasters
  • Adjusting the date is no problem
